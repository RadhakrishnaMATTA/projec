What Happens Inside Your Body After Blood Donation?

Step 1: Blood is drawn from your body, which takes just a few minutes.

Step 2: Your body starts to regenerate the lost blood cells within hours, and full recovery usually takes a few weeks.

Step 3: After donation, you'll feel good knowing you've helped someone in need, and your health may improve due to the regenerative process.
