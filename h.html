HOW TO DONATE BLOOD

Your contribution can save lives!

Eligibility to Donate Blood

- Age: 18–65 years.

- Weight: At least 50 kg (110 lbs).

- No recent illnesses or infections.

- Time since last donation: 12-16 weeks.

How to Prepare for Blood Donation

  1. Eat a healthy meal rich in iron before donating.
  2. Stay hydrated with plenty of water.
  3. Avoid alcohol and caffeine for 24 hours prior.
  4. Get a good night's sleep.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How long does it take to donate blood?

The donation process takes about 10-15 minutes.

2. How often can I donate blood?

You can donate blood once every 8 weeks, or up to 4 times a year.

3. Is blood donation safe?

Yes, blood donation is a safe and controlled process. You will be monitored before, during, and after donation.
